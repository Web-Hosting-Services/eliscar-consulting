ACCOUNTING SERVICES

Assistance to bookkeepers

If you employ a bookkeeper we can assist you in evaluating and managing this task effectively. This will improve your financial reporting and limit the risk of non-compliance.

Processing

We use Sage Accounting for outsourced processing and bookkeeping. We can assist you in keeping all your accounting records up to date.

Annual financial statements

We compile financial statements for your business, ensuring that your financials are in accordance with International Framework and Reporting Standards (IFRS) for SMEs or any other relevant reporting framework.

Management accounts

We offer monthly or bi-monthly balance sheets, income statement comparisons, customised reports depending on management needs, and detailed VAT reports.

Financial Management

We help you to understand your financial results, pointing out risk and potential, so you get a deeper insight into your business.

Payroll Services

Payslips

We provide these to you in your preferred format and offer processing on a weekly, fortnightly or monthly basis

EMP201 Monthly Returns

We assist you with submissions on SARS e-filing

UIF Monthly Returns

As part of our payroll service we assist with the necessary submissions

Payroll Reporting

We supply standard payroll reports

EMP501 Recons

Bi-annual completion and submission.

Bulk Salary Payments

We load the net salary payments onto SAGE Pay, allowing you to make one bulk payment into this account, which ensures the privacy of the payment and saves you time.

IRP5 Tax Certificates

We submit all employee tax certificates on an annual basis, and we can also do manual submissions where necessary

SECRETARIAL SERVICES

CIPC

We interact with the CIPC on your behalf for all its main functions.

Company Registrations

We register companies on your behalf, including the completion, lodging and follow ups of all COR forms, compilation and submission of MOIs, preparation of Share Certificates for the first allotment, an electronic company register and a permanent file for your company that is kept at our offices.

Annual Returns and Annual Duties

We do this directly online and provide this service on a once-off or yearly basis.

Deregistrations

We are able to apply for deregistration on behalf of clients as well as deregister all statutory registrations that may be required of the company/close corporation.

Reinstatements

We provide, request and submit all the necessary information and documentation on your behalf to reinstate your company or close corporation. We have been very successful in all our applications for reinstatements.

Company Registers

We provide clients with an electronic company register of all their company information which may be requested at any time.

Company Amendments

We are able to assist with the preparation, lodgement and follow up of any such changes at the CIPC.

TAX SERVICES

Tax Clearance Certificates

We do a complete status check with SARS for all registered taxpayers, and will provide you with a complete list of outstanding affairs or confirmation that tax affairs are in good order.

Provisional Tax Returns (IRP6)

We submit these for you at the required bi-annual interval.

Annual Tax Returns

We prepare and submit annual tax returns on your behalf.

Advisory

We can consult and advise on any tax issue you or your organisation might have. Our team of qualified accountants and tax specialists has more than 40 years' experience in the industry and stays abreast of all the latest tax developments and implications.

Objections and Disputes

Should you have been assessed incorrectly by SARS for whatever reason, we are able to assist and lodge any objection and dispute with the necessary supporting documentation and reasoning to enable SARS to correctly re-assess any return.

Dividend Tax Returns

We assist with the declaration of dividends to SARS.

Compromise Applications

We assist and consult on the preparation, compiling and submission of compromise applications for Section 200 applications or a down payment arrangement
